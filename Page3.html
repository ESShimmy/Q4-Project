PSY

Psy or Park Jae Sang is a South Korean singer. He was born on December 31, 1977; he is now 30 years old. He had his debut on January 12, 2001. When people hear the name of “Psy”, the song Gangnam style goes right in their mind. Gangnam style made him really famous. This song not only made him famous, but it also made Kpop famous. Psy or Gangnam style technically started the fame of Kpop among the foreign countries. Gangnam style now has 50 billion views on YouTube and over 40 billion listens on Spotify.
